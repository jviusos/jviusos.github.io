SageMath

"SageMath (System for Algebra and Geometry Experimentation) is a mathematical software with features covering many aspects of mathematics, including algebra, combinatorics, numerical mathematics, number theory, and calculus."


Contibutions and complements developped for SageMath:
  • Calculation of the (local) Igusa and Topological zeta functions of a non-degenerated polynomial with respect to his Newton polyhedron. (for Sage v6.10) [Submitting in next versions of SageMath in progress]

  • Filtration and dynamics of logarithmic vector fields of line arrangements in the plane.
