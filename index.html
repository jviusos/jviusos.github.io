Juan Viu-Sos

Ph.D in Mathematics, currently in a post-doc position at IMPA - Instituto de Matemática Pura e Aplicada (Rio de Janeiro, Brazil) with a CAPES/PNPD grant.

Research Interests:
  • Line arrangements: combinatorics vs topology and logarithmic vector fields.
  • Singularity theory: topology, motivic integration and zeta functions.
  • Number theory: effective periods.
  • Low-dimensional topology: knot theory and braids.
  • Computational algebra.
